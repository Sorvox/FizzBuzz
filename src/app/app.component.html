Dear Developer, we would like to ask you to program a simple task for us. Purpose of this request is to test your problem-solving skills and the readability and maintainability of your solution. The solution can be in any language of your choice.

Task Description:

Purpose of the software is to automate the child game “Fizz Buzz” for numbers 1 to 100. The outcome of the script would be the Fizz Buzz results.

Fizz buzz is a group word game for children to teach them about division. Players take turns to count incrementally, replacing any number divisible by three with the word "fizz", and any number divisible by five with the word "buzz". For example:

1, 2, Fizz, 4, Buzz, Fizz, 7, 8, Fizz, Buzz, 11, Fizz, 13, 14, Fizz Buzz, 16, 17, Fizz, 19, Buzz, Fizz, 22, 23, Fizz, Buzz, 26, Fizz, 28, 29, Fizz Buzz, 31, 32, Fizz, 34, Buzz, Fizz, ...

Note: Players taking turns is not applicable. A simple for loop from 1 to 100 is sufficient.
